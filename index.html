How to play?

Objective: Answer multiple-choice questions correctly to score points and use your points to purchase helpful items from the shop.

Game Flow:

  1. Question Format:
    • Each question has 3 answer options, but only 1 correct answer.
    • You have 20 seconds to choose your answer.
  2. Scoring:
    • Correct Answer: You earn 20 points.
    • Wrong Answer or Time Expired: You lose 10 points.
  3. Shop:
    • Use your points to buy items from the shop.
    • The shop offers 6 different items that can help you in the game.
Remember: Stay sharp, think fast, and use your points wisely!
